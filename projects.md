### [Kara Technologies](https://kara.tech){:target="_blank"}

At Kara, I lead product and technical execution of an AI-powered Sign Language avatar translation platform. The core system translates written or spoken language into animated Sign Language in real-time, with approximately 10-second latency.

### Product & Technical Ownership

* Own end-to-end architecture across NLP, structured translation frameworks, runtime animation systems, human-computer interfaces, and cloud APIs.
* Designed a proprietary notation and translation framework with governance and versioning for reliable iteration.
* Delivered around 75% translation understandability on general tasks and near 100% correctness in critical domains, including public emergency messaging.
* Maintain product direction by benchmarking new AI approaches against quality, latency, and cost targets.
