            <p>
            I am the Co-founder and CTO of Kara Technologies (<a href='https://kara.tech' target='_blank'>kara.tech</a>). Since December 2017, I have led the design and delivery of our AI-powered Sign Language translation platform, converting written or spoken language into animated Sign Language via digital avatars in real-time.
            </p>
            <p>
            My role spans end-to-end technical ownership across NLP, translation frameworks, runtime animation systems, human-computer interfaces, and cloud APIs. Our production system delivers approximately 10-second translation latency, around 75% understandability on general translation tasks, and near 100% correctness in critical domains such as public emergency messaging.
            </p>
            <p>
            I build and lead multidisciplinary teams across AI/ML, software engineering, graphics, linguistics, and infrastructure. I also lead enterprise technical discovery and delivery, contributing to over $1M in signed contracts and projects delivered or in progress.
            </p>
            <p>
            Beyond product delivery, I lead security and compliance workstreams including ISO 27001 certification and SOC 2 readiness, and I drive IP strategy including a <a href="https://patents.google.com/patent/US12062125B2/en" target="_blank">granted patent in the US, UK, and Australia</a> for animated sign language sentence generation.
            </p>
            <p>
            I own technical, engineering, and project delivery budgets, and have supported technology diligence and planning across four capital raises.
            </p>
            <p>
            I completed a Master of Science in Computer Science (Artificial Intelligence) at Georgia Institute of Technology (2023-2025), and previously earned a Bachelor of Engineering &amp; Commerce Conjoint at the University of Auckland (2014-2018), graduating with First Class Honors in Computer Systems Engineering and majors in Finance and Management.
            </p>
